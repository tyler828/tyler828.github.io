BIRD FACTS

Birds are a diverse and fascinating group of animals that capture our imagination with their beautiful feathers, intricate songs, and incredible flight abilities. From the majestic bald eagle to the colorful parrots of the tropics, birds come in all shapes and sizes. They can be found in almost every habitat on Earth, from the icy reaches of the Arctic to the tropical rainforests of South America. Birds play important roles in ecosystems as pollinators, seed dispersers, and predators of insects and small mammals. Their migratory journeys span thousands of miles, inspiring wonder and awe in people around the world. Through their unique adaptations and behaviors, birds continue to captivate us and remind us of the incredible diversity of life on our planet.
